How To Use This Webpage

Welcome to The Frag Vault!

Basic Navigation

  • Use the search bar to find fragrances quickly.
  • Tap the buttons to sort the list.
  • The Start Over button also clears selections

Selecting Items

  • Check the box next to available items.
  • Change quantity as needed.
  • Click the green Total button to review and copy your selection.

Viewing Details

  • Tap any row (not checkbox or quantity) to see full details, ratings, and notes.
  • These are my personal comments and ratings. Performance considers both silliage and hours in a scent bubble.
  • I do not consider the time a fragrance is a skin scent as performance.
Happy Fragrance Hunting ✨
